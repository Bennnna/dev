About

I'm Jiwoo Jung

M.S., Department of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, Dongguk University, Seoul.

AI Researcher specializing in Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models, multi-agent systems, and autonomous driving. Published at CVPR 2026 and in SCIE Q1 journals (Knowledge-Based Systems, Complex & Intelligent Systems).

Research focuses on inference-time reasoning for VLA with Visual Chain-of-Thought and Tree-of-Thought evaluation, debate-based multi-agent routing for Vision-Language Model Mixture-of-Experts, and physics-informed neural networks combined with contrastive learning for battery state estimation.

Experienced in building end-to-end AI pipelines with PyTorch, FastAPI, and Docker for real-time model serving. Contributed to government-funded research projects including AI 운전능력평가 표준화사업(KIPoT/경찰청), 인공지능융합혁신인재양성(IITP/MSIT), and ICAN(ICT Challenge and Advanced Network of HRD).
